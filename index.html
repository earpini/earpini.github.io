02 / Writing

Where AI Lands

My Substack about where transformative AI lands in the real world — work, industry, energy, climate, security, public institutions, geopolitics, natural resources, power — and how to talk about those impacts concretely enough that people and policymakers act.

04 / About

I build the bridge between good ideas and real-world results.

Before turning to AI governance, I spent nine years at the World Resources Institute — from intern to Director of Strategy and Impact at WRI Brasil. Across nine countries, I designed strategies, theories of change, and learning systems to connect climate research with policy, coalitions, and institutional action.

That experience taught me that evidence alone rarely changes reality. It has to become strategy, institutions, coalitions, and political urgency.

I'm open to collaborations, advisory work, and consulting: strategy and theory of change, results and learning systems, fundraising cases, and getting governance ideas adopted. If that overlaps with what you're building, email me.
