I work on impact and mobilization strategies to make AI governance real, and write about it at Where AI Lands.

There I learned that research alone doesn't change reality. That's why I focus on where transformative AI lands in the real world — and how to move people, institutions, and policymakers to action.

I'm now co-founding Plures to further develop the AI governance field in middle power countries.
